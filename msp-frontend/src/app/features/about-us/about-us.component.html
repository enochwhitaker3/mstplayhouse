About Main Street Playhouse

History of the Playhouse

Started in 2014 by Judean and Blake Parkinson, the Main Street has become a cherished, locally run non-profit theater nestled in the heart of the Bear River Valley. What started as a passion project for Judean and Blake quickly grew into a beloved cultural hub, where neighbors come together to enjoy high-quality performances, from classic plays to contemporary works. With a deep commitment to fostering local talent and providing affordable entertainment, the Main Street Playhouse continues to be a beacon of creativity and community spirit.
